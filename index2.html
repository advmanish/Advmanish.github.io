About Me

Advocate Manish Sharma (BBA, LL.B., LL.M.) has been practicing law in Rajsamand district, Rajasthan since 2009.
Runs an education and legal awareness YouTube channel.
Practiced for a year at Rajasthan High Court, Jodhpur, and served as Panel Advocate at Shrinath Solitaire Pvt. Ltd.
Guest faculty at Law College.
Specializing in litigation, civil, revenue, and criminal law, and providing advisory services both online and offline.
Has handled cases involving bank searches and property disputes, and offers classes for judiciary aspirants.
Languages: Hindi & English | Available for consultations, teaching, and litigation across Rajasthan courts.
